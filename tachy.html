BeatSense

Python-Based Arrhythmia Detection

Research Design

The research design for BeatSense follows an experimental and computational approach focusing on the analysis of ECG (Electrocardiogram) signals using Python. The system processes ECG waveform data, detects R-peaks, and classifies heart rhythm patterns into normal or abnormal categories based on extracted features such as heart rate, RR intervals, and signal irregularities.

The algorithm is implemented using Python libraries such as NumPy and SciPy for signal processing, while Matplotlib is used for ECG visualization. The study simulates various cardiac conditions — including Bradycardia, Normal Sinus Rhythm, and multiple Tachycardia forms — to assess the system’s capability to differentiate arrhythmias.
